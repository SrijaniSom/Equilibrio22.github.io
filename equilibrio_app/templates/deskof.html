Vice-chancellor
Throughout the year we organise many academic, non-academic programs, conferences, workshops, etc. and invite eminent experts from national and global platforms. Equilibrio techfest is one such flagship program which the institute has organized every year since 2013. It provides a plethora of opportunities for the students to showcase their talent by competing with students across the country. It not only helps them in honing their professional skills but also imparts social values in them.With the aim of not only producing think tanks from our country, Equilibrio nurtures such minds who can challenge real-life problems and develop solutions for them. Promoting scientific and technological advancements along with helping to create awareness about various social and environmental issues has been Equilibrioâ€™s motto.

Registrar
Overcoming various challenges, our college is encouraging youth to achieve brilliance in their fields by showcasing their marvelous ideas and skills in the tech-fest. The catalytic soft-skills training which Equilibrio offers helps the students to polish their personalities with tools of preparedness, strategies and guidance by mentors and seniors. In this festival, students can showcase their creativity and intellect by competing with students from different colleges. Engineering is a journey of four years that is incomplete without fun and learning. It is truly said that the tech-fest of a college is a resemblance of the college in itself. I welcome the students to embark on a journey of success guided by our fellow professors and the best team equipped with a mountain of experience.

Dean
It's my pleasure to welcome you to the EQUILIBRIO - technical festival & community. I hope you will find this festival enthralling, exciting, and invigorating. When I walk through the corridors of this institute, the enthusiasm of our students captivates me, the expertise of our staff, and the uniqueness of what we, as an institute, offer our community makes me feel proud. We work to foster such an environment that is supportive and encouraging for every stakeholder. I hope you have the same sense of joyful discovery as you navigate this site. Equilibrio has made a huge foray into the noble field of science, technology, and management covering from history to the emerging areas.

Teacher coordinator
The journey of Equilibrio started in 2013 and it has successfully travelled its destination over the last years. This has been an amazing platform that not only assists the students in becoming technically sound but also has a significant role in adding social values to their life. It provides the students with an opportunity to ameliorate their ideas, opinions, perception and make them more inventive. This event will promote and encourage the interest of the students in coming up with ideas to solve real-life problems, be it technical or managerial. With this festival, we not only celebrate technology but also promote social causes like education, health, clean energy, etc. among the youth.
